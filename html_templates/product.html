The pants are comfortable to wear and professional looking. The waist runs slightly larger than size, I'm not sure if that's by design though. I like the laminated cargo pocket flaps, which stay up once they are opened. You wouldn't think this is much of a convenience but it is. the placement of the additional 4 side pockets provide extra storage without interfering with the cargo pockets. The navy color matches my uniform top almost exactly. The dividers within the cargo pockets are great. They are about 3"x5" which is perfect for my iphone 4, and a 3x5 notepad.

Some things I wish could be different are the 4 additional pockets run really deep, I guess it is not so much of a problem to run a little too deep because I am going to sew one or two of them shorter. I would have liked to have the two rear pockets open up diagonally instead of having the horizontal openings, It is just easier to access when wearing a duty belt.

all-in-all they are a reasonable priced and well thought out design.
